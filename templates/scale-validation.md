## Phase 5 Go/No-Go Decision

### Scale Readiness Checklist

**Unit Economics:**
- [ ] LTV/CAC > 3
- [ ] CAC payback < 12 months
- [ ] Gross margin > 70%
- [ ] NRR > 100%

**Growth Metrics:**
- [ ] Organic growth > 20% MoM
- [ ] Activation rate > 40%
- [ ] Day 7 retention > 30%
- [ ] Referral program working (>10% rate)

**Operational:**
- [ ] 6+ months runway
- [ ] Repeatable sales process
- [ ] Customer success team established
- [ ] Key hires identified

**Market:**
- [ ] Clear positioning established
- [ ] Case studies published
- [ ] Category leadership emerging

### Scalability Verdict

**READY TO SCALE if:**
- [ ] All unit economics pass
- [ ] Growth metrics trending up
- [ ] Capital secured or self-sufficient
- [ ] Team ready to grow

**OPTIMIZE FIRST if:**
- [ ] CAC too high
- [ ] Retention needs improvement
- [ ] Unit economics not working

### Next Phase Planning

**If READY for Expansion:**
- [ ] Adjacent market identified
- [ ] Product line extensions mapped
- [ ] International expansion plan
