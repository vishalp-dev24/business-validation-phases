## Phase 1 Go/No-Go Decision

### GO Criteria (Need 3+)
- [ ] ILI > 10% from targeted traffic
- [ ] Affordable loss clearly defined and acceptable
- [ ] Initial partner committed
- [ ] Bird-in-Hand inventory shows founder-product fit
- [ ] Users asking "when can I buy?"
- [ ] Evidence problem exists and is painful

### NO-GO Signals (Any disqualifies)
- [ ] ILI < 5% after 200 visitors
- [ ] Affordable loss exceeds risk tolerance
- [ ] No potential customers in network
- [ ] No unique insight or advantage
